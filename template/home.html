Welcome to the home page of the app.

This is deep learning based web application for detecting diseases with medical images.

Overview

In todays time we see the shortage of the doctors in the world especially in India. A lot of people are suffering a lot without the help of the proper medical checkup. Also most of the cases many cases arise leading to dealth due to lack of timely medical checkup

So to cope up with all of those problems this app is designed which would prove its benefits upto much extent.

Application

  • To extend the role of the technology in the medical field
  • To reduce the dependencies on the doctors
  • To help the common people with the normal medical checkup
  • To reduce the cost of diagnosis
